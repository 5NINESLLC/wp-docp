If they received the email then your job is done! If they did not receive the email then you will have to fix outgoing email before inviting more users. Here are a few things to check:

  • Is the email caught in their spam filter?
  • Is there a firewall blocking outgoing email on this server?
  • Is this server incorrectly using a Remote/Local Mail Exchanger?
  • Are you running this app in a sandbox environment that has outgoing email disabled?
