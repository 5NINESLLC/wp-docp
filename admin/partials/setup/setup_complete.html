Next Steps:

  1. Send the app's url to your Program Directors and ask them to register.
  2. Each Program Director should create their program(s) in the app and invite users.
  3. Follow up with each invitee and make sure they registered their account AND have logged in.
  4. Train your faculty to use this app when performing Direct Observation of Clinical Care!

Important notices regarding maintenance and administration:

Please note that you are responsible for maintaining and administering your Wordpress installation and hosting environment -- The DOCC plugin is simply one among many plugins to your Wordpress installation. You can read more about maintaining Wordpress here.

Part of maintaining your Wordpress installation includes periodically installing updates from third-party plugins. These updates will occassionally conflict with the DOCC plugin, breaking functionality. It is your responsibility to maintain and restore a backup of your environment if this happens. You can read more about backing up Wordpress here.

You are responsible for securing your Wordpress installation and all user-submitted data and we insist that you do not use the app to store sensitive information until you are confident that the data is secure. You can read more about securing Wordpress here.

If you have questions about any of these notices then please feel free to reach out to us at support@design.garden for assistance.
